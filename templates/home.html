Welcome!

Welcome to the Student Solidarity committee's website! This committee was created to address many of the prevalent social issues within present day society, and acknowledge the identities and struggles of the individuals within our school. What Do We Do?

Thanks to the generosity of our school community, we've supported several institutions such as the Today Center. We also research various issues and current events in order to be a source of reliable information for our fellow students!

Checkout our issues page to view our research and look at our blog to stay up to date on our activies!
